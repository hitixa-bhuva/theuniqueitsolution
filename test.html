Tiles Finders – A Tile-Only Visual Platform

Tiles Finders is a unique visual discovery platform inspired by Pinterest, built specifically for the tile industry. The goal was to create a centralized space for manufacturers, wholesalers, and dealers to showcase and manage tile products.

Platform Features

Advanced filters
3D visualization
360° views
Inquiry system
Role-based access
Admin dashboard

Impact

Tiles Finders offers a modern and seamless experience for discovering tiles, empowering businesses to reach new customers and streamlining product exploration and inquiry.
